Top 5 Startups in Hyderabad

Neeman's

Founders: Amar Preet Singh and Taran Chhabra
Funding Received: $9.8 million

This Hyderabad-based startup that creates men's shoes from Merino sheep wool is less well-known but no less intriguing. You must be wondering why this is so unique at this point. Let us explain. Only in the extremely cold climates of New Zealand and Australia can this particular breed of sheep survive. We won't get into the specifics, but it took the Neeman's founders roughly 22 months to design, produce, and introduce the first lines of shoes in India. It is not surprising that this shoe-making business is succeeding in Hyderabad's startup scene.

GrabOn

Founders: Ashok Reddy and Bhanu P Raju
Funding Received: $250k

GrabOn is a well-known Indian coupons and deals marketplace that signs up customers and merchants to provide its users with the best offers and coupons online. Inspirelabs Solutions Pvt Ltd, the parent company, was founded back in 2013. One of the top startups in Hyderabad, GrabOn, bootstrapped its way to profitability in the first 15 months.

Darwinbox

Founders:Rohit Chennamaneni, Chaitanya Peddi, and Jayant Paleti
Funding Received: $107 million

This Hyderabad-based startup company is based on the Management technology sector. It allows companies to find and train employees. It also allows the company manager to examine the performance of the employees. It has several best features such as HR workflows, employee engagement, payroll management, etc.

Skinkraft

Founders: Chaitanya Nallan, Veerendra Shivhare, and Sangram Simha
Funding Received: NA

Because every skin type is unique, so too should the skincare products used on it. Purchasing any face wash or cream at random from the grocery store could end up damaging your skin more than helping it. This new business in Hyderabad sells customised skincare kits for as little as Rs 999. If you feel that it is excessive, keep in mind that they have spent more than 10,000 hours researching the best ingredients for your skin. Each of their products incorporates the knowledge of dermatologists from India, Taiwan, and Japan, giving them a stellar reputation among startup businesses in Hyderabad.

UrbanKisaan

Founders:Sairam Reddy, Srinivas Chaganti, Vihari Kanukollu
Funding Received: $1.5 million

UrbanKisaan grows pesticide-free produce in ‘soil-less’ vertical farms, located in cities using 95% ‘less water’ managing everything from the seeds to its proprietary, farm-controlling software system and also empowers people to grow their own safe, fresh and high quality food.Taking a tech-centric approach, they have formulated multiple nutrients for both Indian and exotic vegetables and fruits. Their farming technique allows plants to grow without soil, using only water mixed nutrients enabling farms to move into these abandoned spaces in cities and create new jobs and industry.
